The Global Summit brings together the community of experts, academics, and activists who comprise the Lorem Ipsum affiliate network in a different country every two years.
This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advance the cause of free culture and knowledge.
So if you're active and engaged in the worlds of open contect and knowledge - free software advocates, Wikipedians, Open Knowledger, galleries, libraries, museums, archives, governments and foundations, lawyers and activists - we hope you'll consider joining us this year.
